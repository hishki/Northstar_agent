# Support Handbook
Effective date: 2026-06-01

## Support channels

Starter customers receive email support.

Business customers receive email and chat support.

Enterprise customers receive email, chat, and phone support.

## Support hours

Starter support operates Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time, excluding company holidays.

Business support operates 24 hours a day from Monday 00:00 UTC through Friday 23:59 UTC.

Enterprise support operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

## Technical account management

A shared technical account management pool is available to Business customers who purchase the Premium Support add-on.

A dedicated technical account manager is included only with Enterprise Plus contracts unless separately listed in the customer order form.

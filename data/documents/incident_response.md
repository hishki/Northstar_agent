# Incident Response Policy
Effective date: 2026-03-20

## Severity levels

SEV-1: Complete service outage, confirmed data loss, or a critical security incident affecting multiple customers.

SEV-2: Major degradation or a security issue with limited customer impact.

SEV-3: Minor degradation or isolated feature malfunction.

## Initial response targets

Enterprise:
- SEV-1: 15 minutes
- SEV-2: 1 hour
- SEV-3: 4 business hours

Business:
- SEV-1: 1 hour
- SEV-2: 4 business hours
- SEV-3: 1 business day

Starter:
- Best effort during business hours

## Customer communication

For SEV-1 incidents, Enterprise customers receive updates every 30 minutes until mitigation. Business customers receive updates every 60 minutes.

## Post-incident reports

Enterprise customers receive a written post-incident report within five business days for SEV-1 incidents. Business customers may request a summary.

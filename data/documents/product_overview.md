# Northstar Cloud Product Overview
Effective date: 2026-01-15

Northstar Cloud is a hosted analytics platform for enterprise teams.

## Core capabilities

The platform provides dashboarding, scheduled reports, role-based access control, audit logs, and data connectors.

## Data export

Administrators may export dashboard data in CSV and JSON formats. PDF export is available only on Business and Enterprise plans.

## Availability

The public service target is 99.9% monthly uptime for Business customers and 99.95% monthly uptime for Enterprise customers. Starter plans do not include a contractual uptime commitment.

## Regions

Northstar Cloud is available in the United States and European Union regions. Data residency is selected at account creation and cannot currently be changed without a migration project.

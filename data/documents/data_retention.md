# Data Retention and Deletion
Effective date: 2026-05-05

## Active subscriptions

Customer content remains available while the subscription is active, subject to the customer's configured retention settings.

## After cancellation

Starter and Business customer content is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

Enterprise customer content is retained for 90 days after cancellation unless the signed order form specifies a different period.

## Deletion requests

Verified administrators may request early deletion. Northstar Cloud begins deletion within seven days and completes deletion from primary systems within 30 days. Backup copies expire according to the backup-retention schedule.

## Legal holds

Deletion may be delayed when a valid legal hold applies.
